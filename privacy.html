Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies only to hardik-pandey.com (the portfolio site hosted on GitHub Pages). Subdomains, email services, and other services operating under the hardik-pandey.com domain are separate and may have their own privacy policies. I am not responsible for their data practices.

2. Who I am

This is the personal portfolio site of Hardik Pandey. I am the data controller for any personal data processed through this site. I am based in the United Kingdom and am originally from India.

3. What data is collected and why

2.1 GoatCounter Analytics

This site uses GoatCounter, an open-source, privacy-friendly analytics service. GoatCounter does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. It records anonymised page view data including:

  • The page URL visited
  • The referrer (if any)
  • The visitor's browser and operating system (User-Agent header)
  • A coarse geolocation (country level only, derived from IP, rounded to reduce precision)
  • Screen size

IP addresses are collected transiently by GoatCounter for the purpose of country geolocation and are not stored beyond the request lifecycle. No browsing history, personal identifiers, or fingerprinting techniques are used.

Legal basis (UK GDPR): Legitimate interest — I use this data to understand which pages are most visited and improve the site accordingly. The impact on your privacy is minimal as no personal data is retained.

2.2 IP Address Widget (Cyber page)

On the Cyber Security page, there is an optional widget that can display your public IP address and approximate location. This widget is opt-in — no data is fetched until you click the "Reveal" button. When activated, data is retrieved from either ipapi.co or ipinfo.io (failover). The data is displayed to you in your browser and is not stored, logged, or transmitted to me. It is processed solely on your device.

Legal basis (UK GDPR): Consent — you explicitly choose to reveal this information by clicking the button.

2.3 Cloudflare CDN

This site is proxied through Cloudflare, which acts as a content delivery network and reverse proxy. Cloudflare processes IP addresses and connection metadata as part of its normal operation (routing, caching, and DDoS protection). Cloudflare's processing is governed by its privacy policy and the Cloudflare Data Processing Addendum.

2.4 Email Contact

When you click the email link on the Contact page, your default email client opens with a pre-filled recipient address. Any data you choose to send (your email address, message content, etc.) is transmitted directly between you and me via email. This site does not process, store, or handle that data. The email itself is stored in my email account and retained in line with standard email retention practices.

2.5 Service Worker (PWA Caching)

This site uses a service worker to cache static assets for offline functionality. This operates entirely within your browser and does not transmit any data to me or to any third party.

4. Cookies

This site does not use cookies. GoatCounter is cookie-free, and no other tracking mechanisms are employed. The privacy banner dismissal state is stored in localStorage (a browser-side storage mechanism that does not send data to any server).

5. Data sharing

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. The third-party services used by this site (GoatCounter, ipapi.co, ipinfo.io, and Cloudflare) process data as described above under their respective privacy policies. Each is a independent data controller or data processor as applicable.

6. International transfers

Your data may be processed outside the United Kingdom and India, including in the European Union and the United States, where the third-party services above operate. Where required by applicable law, appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions) are in place.

7. Data retention

  • GoatCounter: Anonymised analytics data is retained indefinitely in aggregate form. IP addresses are not stored.
  • IP widget: No data is retained server-side by me or by the third-party APIs beyond the request lifecycle.
  • Cloudflare: Logs are retained in accordance with Cloudflare's data retention policies (typically 24 hours for edge logs, unless configured otherwise).
  • Email: Emails are retained for the duration of the correspondence and deleted when no longer needed.

8. Your rights

8.1 Under UK GDPR (if you are in the UK or EEA)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data I hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Restriction — request restriction of processing
  • Data portability — request transfer of your data in a structured format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at contact@hardik-pandey.com. I will respond within the timeframe required by law (usually 30 days). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the UK.

8.2 Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)

If you are in India, you have rights including the right to:

  • Access — obtain information about personal data processed
  • Correction and Erasure — update or delete your personal data
  • Grievance redressal — raise concerns about data processing
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent

For any grievances, contact me at contact@hardik-pandey.com. If you are not satisfied with my response, you may approach the Data Protection Board of India.
